Quizzes

  • There will be 25 quizzes administered throughout the term, each worth 1.25 points
    • Of these, 5 quizzes (6.25 points) are provided as bonus opportunities, allowing students to earn extra credit
    • Quizzes will consist of 5–10 multiple choice questions and will cover material from that day’s lecture.

Evaluation Policies

  • All assignments will be administered in Course Den via a LockDown Browser (or equivalent software)
    • Must be taken alone without any other external assistance
    • Requires a computer with webcam and microphone
    • One attempt per assignment
    • Cannot re-enter an assignment after exiting
    • Any failure to meet LockDown Browser requirements or any suspected cheating/suspicious behavior will result in a zero on the exam and may lead to course failure and expulsion

Evaluation Policies

  • Quizzes
    • Available on the “Due” date and will remain open for 7 days
    • Once begun, must be completed within 30 minutes
  • Exams
    • Available only on the Due date
    • Once begun, must be completed within 75 minutes (or 120 for the final)
    • NEW: You have the option to take this in-person; tentatively on day of exam from 2-5pm in 121 (more details to follow)

Evaluation Policies

  • Late assignments will receive zero points
    • Extensions on assignments will only be considered if accompanied by a letter from a medical professional, student services, the dean or other relevant UWG employees
    • Requests for early access to quizzes or exams will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis
